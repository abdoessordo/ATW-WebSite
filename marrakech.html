Marrakech City

Posted on 10 mai 2020

Presentation

Marrakesh is the fourth largest city in the kingdom of Morroco. It is the capital of the mid-southwestern region of Marrakech-Safi. It is west of the foothills of the Atlas Mountains. Marrakesh is 580 km (360 mi) southwest of Tangier, 327 km (203 mi) southwest of the Moroccan capital of Rabat, 239 km (149 mi) south of Casablanca, and 246 km (153 mi) northeast of Agadir.
